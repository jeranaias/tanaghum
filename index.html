Eyes and Ears Working Together

Research shows that presenting audio and text simultaneously creates stronger memory encoding. Tanaghum leverages this through precise word-level synchronization.

  • Highlighted text follows along with audio playback
  • Click any word to jump to that moment in the audio
  • Vocabulary tooltips with definitions and pronunciation
  • Adjustable playback speed for challenging passages
  • Loop sections for focused practice
